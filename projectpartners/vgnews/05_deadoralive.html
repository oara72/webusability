Dead or Alive 5 Last Round

The final update to Dead or Alive 5 brings the game to PlayStation 4 and Xbox One. Last Round features more characters, stages, and costumes than any other Dead or Alive game to date.

Dead or Alive 5 Last Round is the final revision to Dead or Alive 5, which brings the series to PlayStation 4 and Xbox One for the first time.

The game features all of the characters and stages included in Dead or Alive 5 Ultimate, the DLC characters introduced in Ultimate, as well as two additional characters and stages.
