Forza Horizon 2 Presents Fast & Furious

Forza Horizon 2 Presents Fast & Furious is a standalone expansion for the open world racing game Forza Horizon 2 to promote the latest film in the Fast & Furious movie franchise "Furious 7".

The standalone expansion was made available for free on March 27, 2015 for the Xbox One and Xbox 360. After April 10, 2015, the expansion cost users $10. If a player initiated the download within the two-week window, they can continue playing the game at no charge. Players will not need to own the base game to download the expansion.

In the game the player is recruited to source cars by Fast & Furious' Tej Parker, who will be voiced by Chris “Ludacris” Bridges. The game will feature many special edition cars.

  • 2012 Nissan GT-R Fast & Furious Edition
  • 1998 Toyota Supra Fast & Furious Edition
